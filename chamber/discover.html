Thanksgiving Point

Among the many gems Utah County has to offer, Thanksgiving Point is a must! This indoor/outdoor complex has fun to offer for every age group.

Discover something new at either:
1) the Museum of Anchent Life.
2) The Museum of Natural Curiosity.

Take a stroll through the 55 acers of Asron Gardens, including fish ponds, a waterfall, and the "Light of the World Garden" a lifesize bronz statue garden depicting the life of Christ.

Enjoy these, and much more, at Thanksgiving Point!

Water Parks

Summer in Utah County is quite the splash! Not only do we have over a dozen splashpads and pools to beat the heat, we also have Splash Summit, a water park adventure for all ages!

Relax and float around the park in the Canyon River.

Brace yourself for some crazy waves at Rocky Mountain Beach!

Gather your friend and race to the finishline on this 8-lane, 300-foot, racing slide, Avalanche!

Do you want to chace the addrenalin at the top of Sky Beaker, or relax and float on the waves. With 15 different attractions, there's fun for everyone!

Event Centers

Utah County cultivates networking opertunities. If your business field is home-improvement, jewlery and cosmetics, homeschooling, or something else, there are pleanty of networking oppertunities at the event centers.

Meet up with your friends, get to know local businesses, or demonstraight your own at our popular expo events, such as the Home-Show, the Business Expo, the Womans Expo, and more...

Whether you are there to walk around and meet new people, or you are there to advertise or sell your goods, there is something for everyone!

Shopping Districts

Support the local economy, and have a lot of fun, by stopping by one of Utah County's several Mall's and shopping centers.

If you are looking for something good to eat, there are pleanty of options wherever you go. Noodles, sushi, pizza, gormet sandwichis, and more...

If you are preparing for a party with friends, or a business dinner with your collegues, the clothings options range from forever 21 to Nordstrems. Cloths and exessories for any occation.

Campuses

With Utah County being the home to two major collage campuses, there is a lot of business potential. Whether you are gathering statistics for product marketing, playing off of the collage rivalries to sell merchandice, or your product's target audience is young adults, there is a lot of oppertunity.

But wait, theres more! With two collages, there twice the major events and business oppertunities. Sports games, confrences, plays, recitals, and art exibits. There are many things to enjoy, and the business oppertunities to match.
