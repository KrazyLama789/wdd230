reference the elements/content to which the typography choices will apply

  • Title(h1): Montserrat (semi-bold 600)

    A large, semi-bold title font to catch the eye.

  • Subheadings(h2+): Montserrat (light 300)

    A light subheading to contrast the bold title.

  • Paragraphs(p): Montserrat (regular 400)

    A regular un-ornamented font to make reading easier on longer Paragraphs.
