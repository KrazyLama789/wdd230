About Us

Welcome to Bountiful Foods. We are a small, locally owned business, right here in Costal California! Our initial purpose was to facilitate in getting locally grown organic produce right to the people - to you! Since our conception, we have grown in our reach and capability, so has our purpose.

With such a high demand for fresh organic foods, we have expended what we offer, and how we offer it. While we continue to provide our locally grown organic produce, in addition we now offer custome fruit bags, and more! Don't forget to check out our "Fresh" page, to make your very own Made-to-Order custome fruit drink!

Bountiful Foods is located in Carlsbad Village, Carlsbad California. We have also recently added foodtruck locations in La Jolla, Long Beach, and Huntingtons Beach.
